Final Fantasy XIV Fun Facts

The Rise of the Warrior of Light

Did you know? The Warrior of Light is a central figure in FFXIV, a hero chosen by the Mothercrystal, Hydaelyn. This iconic figure has helped save Eorzea from countless threats, including the mighty primals and the terrifying Garlean Empire!

A Land of Many Wonders

Eorzea is home to a massive variety of landscapes, from the lush and mystical forests of the Shroud to the fiery deserts of Ul'dah! There's always a new adventure waiting just around the corner, whether you're crafting or slaying monsters.

Summon Your Friends (and Enemies!)

Did you know that in FFXIV, you can summon powerful entities called Primals? These creatures are often inspired by gods from various cultures. Whether you're summoning Ifrit to wreak havoc or fighting the fierce Titan, each primal fight is a spectacle.

The Legacy of the Crystal Tower

The Crystal Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in FFXIV's history. Originally part of the game's original storyline, it has grown into a symbol of hope, with its impressive architecture and unique connection to the game's overarching narrative.

Your Very Own Moogle

Everyone loves a Moogle! These adorable creatures are not only cute, but they also play an important role in the game's economy and story. In fact, they often serve as messengers for special events and deliver adorable gifts to players!
